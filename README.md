# How Well Do You Know Your Bestie?

"How Well Do You Know Your Bestie?" is a social-casual "Sync Quiz" experience built on the Roblox platform. The gameplay centers on asynchronous and synchronous choice-matching. Two players (a "duo") are presented with the same series of prompts or preferences. The game calculates a "Compatibility Score" based on the alignment of their inputs, leveraging the natural social desire for validation and competitive friendship testing.

Engagement Loop
The experience utilizes a high-frequency feedback loop:

The Hook: Players invite a high-value social connection (a "Bestie") to validate their relationship.

The Action: Rapid-fire decision-making across various lifestyle and personality categories.

The Variable Reward: A final "Relationship Grade" or "Sync Percentage" that is highly shareable within the Roblox social ecosystem, driving organic growth through peer invitation.

Monetization & Retention Strategy
The game employs a Freemium Content Model:

Base Tier: Broad, general-interest question packs are available for free to maintain high concurrent user (CCU) counts and accessibility.

Premium Tier: Specialized "Themed Packs" (e.g., deeper emotional dives, niche pop culture) are gated behind microtransactions (Robux).

Retention Tactics: The developers utilize "Admin-led Events" and scheduled content drops (Weekend Giveaways) to maintain floor-level engagement and mitigate churn, effectively converting free users into community advocates.
